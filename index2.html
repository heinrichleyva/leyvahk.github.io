To my loved ones,

If you are reading this, this means that you are very important to me and I would like you to know that you made my life worth living. I cannot imagine my life without you and I wouldn't have spent it any other way. You make all the hardships in life bearable and I would like to thank you for that. I wish I have done the same thing for you and I am sorry if I have caused you pain or confusion at times and I will make it up to you. Thank you for everything.

Love, Heinrich
